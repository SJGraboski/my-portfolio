About Me

Writer, reporter, designer, programmer--I wear a slew of hats to fit the job. As a web developer with a background in journalism, I want to employ my coding and communication chops to help creative businesses reach their audiences. My favorite topics: literature, gaming and technology.

I earned my M.S. in digital media at the Columbia School of Journalism in Manhattan, NY. Before that, I graduated with a B.A. in English from Drew University in Madison, NJ.

Since school, I've designed blogs and web pages for artists, small media companies and fellow reporters. Check out the sidebar to see my latest work!
